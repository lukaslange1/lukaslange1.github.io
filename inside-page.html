Working with us

We build solutions for your bioinformatics problems

If you have a bioinformatics problem that needs solving, talk to us. We take on big challenges that require creative thinking, technical skill and scientific insight. We work in close collaboration with you over several months to build a technological solution that will improve your organization. Along the way, we teach you more about the cutting-edge technology we use. At the same, you're investing in our growth by teaching us about your problem. Working with Oxford Phenome means engaging in a close, mutually benefitial partnership.

We have a particular focus on structuring phenotypic information

Having access to structured phenotypic information of patients is an essential key for achieving a high diagnostics yield in genomic studies. However, extracting phenotypic information for large patient cohorts is a problem that every genomics organization, from Beijing to the UK and the US, is struggling with. As genomics researchers working in Oxford, one of the leading centers for genomics in the world, we experience this first-hand, every day. Scalable solutions to solve this problem don't currently exist, which is why we are building one. If you have a similar experience, get in touch with us and we will help you structure your phenotypic information.

We will collaborate closely with you to ensure the maximum benefit for your organization

Projects we operate are jointly run between Oxford Phenome and our partners. For every project, Oxford Phenome and our partners each nominate a project leader. For the duration of the project, both project leaders will work together closely to ensure the best possible result for your organization.

We work according to a well defined timeline:

  1. Setup phase: we will set up a call or meeting with you, scope your problem, define the data to be used and desirable deliverables and define the terms of our collaboration.
  2. Prototyping phase: we will build an initial technical solution and collect feedback from you in close iterative loops.
  3. Refinement phase: we will develop the technical solution to maturity and make it scalable.
  4. Data collection phase: we will use the custom-built technical solution to solve your problem.
  5. Finalization phase: we will hand over the final deliverable and provide your institution with a documentation of our learnings from the project.

Terms

While specific terms are discussed in the setup phase, we will maintain the rights to the intellectual property developed during the collaboration.
