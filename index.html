Making unstructured healthcare data valuable

Over 50% of the data critical for pharma companies and hospitals exists in unstructured text, be it in Electronic Medical Records or radiology and pathology reports. Oxbridge Genomics' AI technology platform "Apogene" finds information crucial to your success in unstructured text and makes it easily accessible for you.

Tech

Apogene is a medical concept recognition engine. Designed to understand the complexity of biomedical text, Apogene identifies key concepts in any document, turning raw text into valuable data. The algorithm seamlessly integrates into your workflow - you won't even notice it's there.

Use cases

Phenotype your cohort

The rapid drop in sequencing costs has spawned a vast number of research studies comprising 100k+ patients (Genomics England, Human Longevity, Precision Medicine Initiative, ...). All large-scale sequencing studies require deep phenotypic data on their patients, which are collected from Electronic Medical Records (EMR). Instead of manually generating phenotypic data from your EMR systems, automate the process using Apogene.


Get ICD-10 codes

Reimbursement in many healthcare systems is done via standardized vocabularies, such as ICD-10. Encoding information in the ICD-10 format is a tedious process for doctors taking away time from their patients. Furthermore, manual encoding is often done incompletely, which leads to hospitals losing money. Apogene automatically mines text on your patients for relevant information and provides your hospital with the correct ICD-10 codes.


Search your files

Hospitals and pharma companies often still work with large numbers of inaccessible documents (scanned PDFs, word documents, E-mail). Sifting through those documents to find information on patients is a time-consuming process. Apogene can easily index those documents and make them searchable, cutting down time lost in the workplace.




Power research apps

Use Apogene's structured output to feed your research pipeline. Apogene outputs the data it finds and all annotations in standardized formats, ready to power your tools.
