About Oxbridge Phenome

Oxford Phenome is a bioinformatics consultancy that helps companies, large research organizations and hospitals make sense of their data. By using deep learning and natural language processing, we help our partners supplement their workflows with information that's already existing in their file systems. In particular, we take unstructured patient information contained in medical records, doctor's notes, emails, etc., and turn it into structured phenotypic information in the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) format. We currently do this for English, Mandarin, Cantonese, Arabic and German.
