About Oxbridge Genomics

Oxbridge Genomics helps companies, large research organizations and hospitals make sense of their data. By using deep learning and natural language processing, we help our partners supplement their workflows with highly valuable information that's already existing in their file systems. In particular, we take unstructured patient information contained in medical records, doctor's notes, emails, etc., and turn it into structured phenotypic information in the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) format.

Deep phenotyping tool

Our core product is a deep phenotyping tool. The tool automatically detects relevant medical information in text and turns it into phenotypic information that is usable for genomic analyses. By using machine learning, our system adapts itself to your database and learns the specific ways in which you express information. For example, it recognizes institution-specific acronyms, synonyms and regionally differing expressions and can relate these to standardized databases. To date, we have processed over 20,000 samples.

Human Phenotype Ontology

We take your unstructured patient records and doctor notes and map all the contained phenotypic information to the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO).

Natural Language Processing

Our team uses natural language processing to parse documents containing phenotypic information.

Deep Learning

By employing state-of-the-art deep-learning techniques, we generate insights from your data to supplement your workflow.
